Roomi

Problem
Living together is hard.
What makes living together so difficult?
From 10 qualitative interviews, we found that people struggle the most with communcation, accountability, equity when living with others.
User Personas
1. Frustrated Frank
2. Lazy Lucy
Low-Fidelity Prototypes
Created low fidelity prototypes of the dashboard, create task page, and calendar page.
Qualtrics Survey
Distributed Qualtrics survey to over 40 participants. Questions had a screenshots of our wireframes with questions below. Participants found the dashboard confusing, wanted optional options for parts of the task form, and wanted a complete visual calendar.
High-Fidelity Prototypes
High fidelity considered user feedback and made changes to our low fidelity prototypes as well as developing them with colors and enhanced components.

Finishing Touches
Changed the colors of the app from blue to orange to make the app more lively and fun. We wanted to making living together fun and easy.
